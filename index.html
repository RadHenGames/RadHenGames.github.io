About Rad Hen Games

Rad Hen Games is an independent game development project dedicated to creating original HTML5 games that anyone can enjoy directly in a web browser. Our games are built with a focus on accessibility, smooth performance, and replayability. Whether you're looking for a quick challenge or trying to beat your previous high score, our goal is to provide an enjoyable experience for players of all ages.

We believe browser games should be easy to access and fun to play without requiring downloads or accounts. As our library grows, you'll find more original titles covering arcade, action, and casual genres.
